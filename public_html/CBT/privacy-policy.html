Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

CBT Companion ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our app.

All subscriptions and payments are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

1. Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers.

All mood entries, thought records, journal entries, and preferences you enter into CBT Companion are stored:

  • Locally on your device
  • Securely in your private iCloud account (if iCloud is enabled)

Because we do not have our own servers, we do not have access to your data and do not collect or sell any personal information. It remains strictly between you and Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure.

2. iCloud Sync

CBT Companion uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across your devices. This process is managed entirely by Apple. You can manage your iCloud data in your device settings under Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Account Storage.

3. Sensitive Data

CBT Companion may store sensitive personal information such as mood logs, thought records, and journal entries. This data is stored exclusively on your device and in your private iCloud account. We cannot access, read, or analyze your entries. Your mental health data is yours alone.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The app is completely ad-free and tracker-free.

5. Children's Privacy

CBT Companion does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Because all data remains on the user's device or private iCloud account, we do not have access to user data regardless of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised 'Last Updated' date.
