Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a thought record?
Tap the "Thought Records" tab in the app and press the "+" button. You'll be guided through identifying the situation, your automatic thoughts, emotions, cognitive distortions, and creating a balanced alternative thought.
Is my data private?
Yes, absolutely. Your data is stored securely in your own private iCloud account. We (the developers) cannot see, access, or sell your data. Your thought records, mood entries, and journal entries stay between you and Apple.
How do I track my mood?
From the Home screen, tap the mood button to quickly log how you're feeling. Choose from five mood levels and optionally add notes about what's happening. Your mood history is visualized in the Insights tab.
How do I use the breathing exercises?
Navigate to the Exercises tab and select "Breathing." Choose a technique like Box Breathing or 4-7-8, then follow the animated guide. You can customize the duration and set a timer for your session.
What are cognitive distortions?
Cognitive distortions are patterns of thinking that are biased or inaccurate. Common examples include "All-or-Nothing Thinking" and "Catastrophizing." The app includes a library of 150+ examples to help you identify and challenge them.
Does the app work on iPad and Mac?
Yes! CBT Companion is available on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Your data syncs seamlessly between all your devices via iCloud, so you can pick up right where you left off.
Is this a replacement for therapy?
No. CBT Companion is a self-help tool designed to complement professional therapy, not replace it. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a mental health professional or your local emergency services.
