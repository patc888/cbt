Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a thought record?
Simply open the app, navigate to the "Thoughts" tab, and tap the "+" button. You'll be guided through the standard 7-step process: Situation, Feelings, Thoughts, Evidence For, Evidence Against, Distortions, and Alternative Thoughts.
What are cognitive distortions?
Cognitive distortions are biased ways of thinking that can exacerbate negative emotions. Cognitive Behavioral Therapy+ helps you identify 15 common distortions, such as Catastrophizing, Black-and-White Thinking, and Mind Reading, with detailed examples.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. Your data is stored securely in your private iCloud account and on your local device. We do not have access to your thoughts, logs, or records. Your privacy is our highest priority.
How do I export my data?
Go to the "Settings" tab and select "Data Export." You can download your full history of thought records and mood logs in CSV format for use in other apps or with your therapist.
Does the app sync between my devices?
Yes! Cognitive Behavioral Therapy+ works on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. All your data syncs automatically through iCloud, so you can start a thought record on your phone and finish it on your Mac.
Is there a cost for the app?
Cognitive Behavioral Therapy+ offers a free version with all essential tools. A premium subscription is available to unlock advanced themes, unlimited thought records, and detailed analytics.
